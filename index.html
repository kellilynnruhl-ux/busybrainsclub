What is body doubling?

"It's like having a gym buddy, but for your to-do list."

For many neurodivergent people, starting a task is the hardest part. Body doubling is a productivity strategy where you work alongside another person to improve focus. It’s not about being watched—it’s about shared presence reducing the executive function load.

Wait… what is body doubling?

Body doubling is a simple trick: work near other people (even virtually) and your brain is more likely to cooperate. You’ll work quietly and independently—just share your goal at the start and a quick update at the end.

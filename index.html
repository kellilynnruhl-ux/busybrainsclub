What is body doubling?

Body doubling is the practice of working alongside other people to create structure, focus, and motivation. Many people find it easier to start tasks, stay on track, and follow through when they are in a shared environment with others who are also working.

These groups are supportive, encouraging, and intentionally low-pressure. Participants do not interact during the work session beyond sharing a goal in the chat at the beginning and a brief update at the end. The sense of encouragement comes from the body doubling process itself: quietly working alongside others who are also focusing on their own tasks. While I am a licensed therapist, these sessions are not therapy, mental health treatment, or clinical services.

Calm Structure

Empowering Support
